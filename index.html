Smash-A-Bug!

For those days when you bang your head on the desk while trying to squash the bugs in your code... when you need to vent your frustration, squash some virtual bugs here!

To play, use the box to set the number of bugs you'd like to squash. Click "Start" and the game will begin! Bugs will continue to appear until you've reached the target you set.
